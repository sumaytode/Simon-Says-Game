Simon Game

Simon Says is an engaging web-based memory challenge that tests your ability to recall and repeat sequences. Players follow the commands of "Simon," recreating patterns of lights that grow progressively complex. It's a fun blend of concentration and reflexes that keeps players hooked as they strive to achieve higher levels.

Instructions for Simon Says Game

  1. Start the Game: Click the "Start The Game" button to begin.
  2. Objective: Memorize and recreate the sequence of colors shown on the screen.
  3. How to Play:
    • Simon will highlight a series of colors in a specific order. Watch carefully.
    • When it's your turn, click the colored buttons in the same sequence Simon displayed.
  4. Level Progression:
    • After successfully completing a sequence, your level will increase.
    • Each new level adds a new color to the sequence, making it more challenging.
  5. Difficulty Increase:
    • As levels progress, the speed of the sequence display will increase.
    • Keep your focus sharp as sequences grow longer and faster.
  6. Game Over:
    • If you make a mistake in recreating the sequence, the game ends.
    • Click the "Start The Game" button again to restart and try to beat your previous score.
  7. Tip: Concentrate, stay calm, and aim for a higher score with each attempt!
